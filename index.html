About Us

Luxxe Escapes is a high-end travel experience offering Villas and Residence stay at Vidanta destinations in Nuevo/Puerto Villarta and Riviera Maya/Cancún. Vidanta locations offer a 5-star plus plus experience that includes special concierge service, one of a kind dining experiences, award winning Greg Norman Designed Golf courses, and private limo service from the airport. Also offered are cruise experiences and private jet accommodations. Our goal is to get to know each and every client, and partner with them to design the ultimate itinerary, taking care of every single detail, making it a memorable experience.
